How to speed up old Android Phone

Posted on: 2021-10-30

This was a problem I had too. I have an old Phone with 1 GB and It is very slow. In this article let me tell you how I speeded up that phone.

Switch to lightweight apps

1. Use Google go apps

Most Android phones have Google Apps like Google Search, Chrome, Gmail, Youtube, Google Maps. Google released go apps (lite apps) for a lot of Google's apps. These Go Apps can run low spec or old Android phones faster. Some Go Apps for normal Apps are given below.

  • Google - Google Go (Download here)

  • Google Maps - Maps go (Download here)

  • YouTube - Youtube Go (Download here)

Change browser

If you have an old phone with low specifications you want a lightweight browser Because sometimes google chrome is stuck when you are browsing the web. I tried different browsers. My personal choice is Via browser. Some lightweight browsers are given below.

  • Via browser - Its size is less than 2MB and It has a lot of cool features. And Via browser is more customizable. (Download here)

  • Opera mini - Lightweight browser with a lot of features. (Download here)

2. Remove unnecessary Apps

You can remove unnecessary Apps and If You have unwanted pre-installed You can Disable Apps.

3. Update Operating system

Updates are bringing improvements if you update your Operating System your device can operate speedily.

Restart Phone

Restart Your Phone When You're not Using it. That helps to speed up Your Phone.
